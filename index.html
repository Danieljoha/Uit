About This Portfolio

This portfolio showcases the final projects and key assignments from my Bachelor's degree in Samfunnsøkonomi med Datavitenskap at UiT. The program combines economic theory with modern data science methods, preparing students to analyze economic phenomena using quantitative methods and programming tools.

Key Skills Demonstrated: Economic modeling, statistical analysis, econometric methods, R programming, data visualization, and research methodology.
